Controllers

They excel in controlling areas of the map through use of smokes and walls to create choke points your team can exploit.

Initiators

They create opportunities for their team to enter sites through information gathering abilities and flashes.

Duelists

They are the first ones to enter sites and look for first blood in order to give your team an early advantage or to make space on a site.

General Game Requirements

As of my last update in January 2022, here are the
recommended system requirements for playing Valorant.

<

  • Wifi/Network

    Valorant requires a stable internet connection with low latency, preferably wired, for smooth gameplay and minimal disruptions.

  • System Requirements

    Valorant's minimum requirements include Windows 7, Intel Core 2 Duo E8400, 4GB RAM, and Intel HD 3000 graphics.

  • Riot Profile

    Valorant requires a Riot Games account with linked Riot ID to play, providing access to competitive play, progress tracking, and social features.

  • Voice Chat

    Valorant voice chat requires a compatible microphone and headset, with voice communication enabled in-game settings.

  • Ping Issue

    Stable internet connection with low latency (<100 ms) for optimal gameplay experience in Valorant.

  • In-Game Purchases

    In-Game Purchases in Valorant may require a supported payment method and an internet connection.
